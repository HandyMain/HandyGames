## üí° New Game Concepts

### 1. Coding Commander (Logic)
*   **Concept:** Guide a robot through a maze using blocks (Forward, Turn Left, Jump).
*   **Learning:** Basic programming concepts, sequencing.

### 2. Dino Dig (World/Science)
*   **Concept:** Use a brush tool (touch drag) to clear dirt layers and reveal dinosaur bones. Then assemble the bones like a puzzle.
*   **Tech:** Canvas masking / erasing effects.

### 3. Grocery Store (Math)
*   **Concept:** You are the cashier. Scan items, calculate the total, and count change.
*   **Learning:** Addition, subtraction, currency handling.

### 4. Human Body (Science)
*   **Concept:** "Operation" style drag-and-drop. Place the heart, lungs, and stomach in the right place on a skeleton.
*   **Feature:** Tap an organ to hear what it does.

### 5. Music Maker (Creative)
*   **Concept:** A simple sequencer or drum pad.
*   **Feature:** "Record" a loop and play along with it.

### 6. Solar System Explorer (World)
*   **Concept:** 3D interactive view of planets.
*   **Feature:** Zoom in to see moons, spin the planets.

### 7. Recycling Hero (Logic)
*   **Concept:** Items move on a conveyor belt. Swipe Left for Trash, Right for Recycle, Up for Compost.
*   **Learning:** Environmental awareness, sorting speed.
